## Scoring Implementation

Layer 6 scoring creates a **Personality and Mindset Profile** across multiple dimensions:

### 1. Growth vs Fixed Mindset Score

**Scoring:**
- Growth-oriented responses: +1 point
- Fixed-oriented responses: -1 point
- Neutral/balanced responses: 0 points

**Classification:**
- **Growth Mindset** (score ≥ 3): Embraces challenges, sees failure as learning
- **Mixed Mindset** (score 0-2): Situational, some growth tendencies
- **Fixed Mindset** (score < 0): Avoids challenges, views abilities as static

**Impact:**
- Growth mindset → More experimental learning paths
- Fixed mindset → Structured, proven learning methods with high support

---

### 2. Risk Tolerance Score

**Scoring:**
- High risk responses: +2 points
- Moderate risk responses: +1 point
- Low risk responses: 0 points
- Risk-avoidant responses: -1 point

**Classification:**
- **High Risk Tolerance** (score ≥ 8): Bold, experimental, comfortable with uncertainty
- **Moderate Risk Tolerance** (score 4-7): Strategic, calculated risks
- **Low Risk Tolerance** (score < 4): Cautious, prefers proven methods

**Impact:**
- High tolerance → Advanced strategies, cutting-edge tactics
- Low tolerance → Conservative approaches, step-by-step validation

---

### 3. Extraversion vs Introversion

**Scoring:**
- Extraverted responses: +1 point
- Introverted responses: -1 point
- Balanced responses: 0 points

**Classification:**
- **Extraversion** (score ≥ 2): Energized by interaction, collaborative
- **Balanced** (score -1 to 1): Ambivert, context-dependent
- **Introversion** (score ≤ -2): Energized by solitude, reflective

**Impact:**
- Extraverted → Group learning, peer discussions, live sessions
- Introverted → Self-paced, independent study, written materials

---

### 4. Adaptability Score

**Scoring:**
- High adaptability: +2 points
- Moderate adaptability: +1 point
- Low adaptability: 0 points

**Classification:**
- **Highly Adaptable** (score ≥ 5): Thrives on change, flexible
- **Moderately Adaptable** (score 2-4): Can adapt with structure
- **Prefers Stability** (score < 2): Values routine and predictability

**Impact:**
- High adaptability → Dynamic content, frequent updates
- Low adaptability → Consistent structure, clear expectations

---

### 5. Energy & Motivation

**Scoring:**
- High energy: +2 points
- Moderate energy: +1 point
- Low energy: 0 points

**Classification:**
- **High Energy** (score ≥ 5): Intensive work bursts, ambitious
- **Moderate Energy** (score 2-4): Steady, consistent pace
- **Conservation Mode** (score < 2): Needs support, smaller steps

**Impact:**
- High energy → Intensive bootcamps, fast-paced content
- Low energy → Gentle pacing, frequent breaks, bite-sized lessons

---

### 6. Innovation Appetite

**Scoring:**
- Highly innovative: +2 points
- Moderately innovative: +1 point
- Conservative: 0 points

**Classification:**
- **Early Adopter** (score ≥ 5): Experiments with new methods
- **Pragmatic** (score 2-4): Tests before adopting
- **Traditional** (score < 2): Sticks to proven methods

**Impact:**
- Early adopter → Cutting-edge strategies, experimental modules
- Traditional → Classic frameworks, time-tested approaches
